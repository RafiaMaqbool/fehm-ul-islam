About the Book

Fehm-ul-Islam (Understanding Islam through Quran) is a compilation of Quranic teachings by Ishtiaq Ahmad Qazi,
arranged to help readers gradually understand the essence of Islam. It highlights faith, worship, social obligations, personal conduct,
and the Quran’s guidance for modern life.

What You’ll Learn

Faith (Iman)

Belief in Allah, His messengers, revealed books, angels, and the Hereafter.

Worship

Prayer, fasting, charity, and pilgrimage as the foundations of spiritual life.

Ethics

Justice, honesty, kindness, forgiveness, and social responsibility.

Science & Quran

Quranic verses that align with scientific truths about creation and the universe.
